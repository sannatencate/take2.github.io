Advice from students to students 

~

W O R K     H A R D      B R E A K      H A R D 

  • Focus on a certain subject for around 2 hours - work for 50 minutes and then break for 10 minutes 
  • Switch up your study location from day to day 
  • Remove distractions! - put your phone in your backpack and make sure your laptop messages are on Do Not Disturb
  • Treat yourself at the end of each section and after a long day!

M A K E      S U R E       Y O U      A R E      G E T T I N G

S L E E P 

  • Try to get 7-8 hours of sleep a day so that you are resting your brain during study days
  • Put away your phone 20 minutes before bed - try meditating or reading a book to calm your mind 
  • Block out your schedule to include adequate sleep and plan to study around it! - not the other way around 

 M A K E       S U R E       Y O U       A R E       F U E L I N G    

  C O R R E C T L Y 

  • Start your day with a nutritious breakfast to boost your energy
  • bring along nutrient-dense snacks to support your levels throughout the day 
  • Have any extra time? Go for a short walk or workout to clear your head
